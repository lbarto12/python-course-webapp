There are also some useful functions for numbers, such as `sum`, `min`, and `max`

sum only works on lists and other iterable structures, but min and max work on any number of parameters

Given the numbers stored in `a` and `b`, print the `sum`, `min` and `max` of the two numbers with:

print(sum([a, b])) print(min(a, b)) print(max(a, b))
