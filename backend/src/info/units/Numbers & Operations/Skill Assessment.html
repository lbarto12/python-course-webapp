We have discussed many operations that can be performed on numbers, now let's put them all together.

perform the following steps on a in this order:

1. add `5` to `a`

2. multiply `a` by `2`

3. subtract `3` from `a`

4. divide `a` by `2`

5. raise `a` to the power of `3`

6. floordiv `a` by `2`

7. `unpack` the result of `divmod` on `a` and `3` into two variables and print them on the same line.

8. print the `binary representation` of the `quotient` returned from the `divmod` call. (you will need to cast the `quotient` to an `int`, you can do so by calling `int` on the `quotient`):

print(bin(int(quotient)))

your answers to `7` and `8` should be on seperate lines.
